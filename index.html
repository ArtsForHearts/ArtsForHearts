Arts for Hearts

Saturday, April 28th, 2018

1:00 - 8:00 PM

Arts for Hearts is the Johns Hopkins American Heart Association performing arts benefit showcase. This official annual event will take place on Saturday, April 28, 2018, from 1:00 - 8:00 PM as a part of JHU Alumni Weekend. Free admission! There will be a huge outdoor stage on the Homewood Beach for Hopkins and Baltimore community dance and musical performances. There will be food trucks, ice cream trucks, tons more food, and raffles to enter for a chance to win Oriole's tickets, gift cards, and much more! Join us for this great outdoor concert for a great cause. 100% proceeds go to the American Heart Association.

Since Arts for Hearts is a part of Alumni Weekend, the registration site for Alumni Weekend can be accessed here: http://alumni.jhu.edu/AW2018. Sign up and join in the fun!
