Arts for Hearts 2017

Arts for Hearts took place on Sunday, April 23, 2017 from 1:00-8:00 PM on the Johns Hopkins University Homewood Beach as a part of Alumni Weekend. Food and beverages from food trucks and raffle tickets were sold. 100% of proceeds went to the American Heart Association.
